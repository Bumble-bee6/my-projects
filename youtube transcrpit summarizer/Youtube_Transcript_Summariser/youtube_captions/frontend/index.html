How to Use

  1. Copy Video Link: Go to YouTube and copy the link of the video you want to summarize.
  2. Paste Link: Paste the copied link into the box at the top of this page.
  3. Pick Summary Type: Select how detailed you want your summary to be: Brief, Standard, or Detailed.
  4. Choose Language: Pick your preferred language for the summary.
  5. Get Summary: Click the Generate Summary button.
  6. View Results: Check the Summary and Full Transcript tabs for your results.
  7. Save or Share: Use the Copy, Save as File, or Open Video buttons as needed.
  8. See History: Find your previous summaries in the History tab.
